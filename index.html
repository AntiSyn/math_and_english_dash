Get Ready to Be the Cool Kid!

This is a fun game created by Allan Hamawand to develop your skills in Maths and English!

Hey future champ! You're in the second lane and are against the nastiest runner of them all, Steve from Minecraft! Get ready to outrun him and the devious creepers lurking in the shadows!

You have two modes to pick from each with three difficulties. Easy for those who are wishing to slowly improve their skills. Medium for those who want to strive and become better than all. Or Hard mode, a difficulty only for the best who have finetuned their skills to the dot!

In the Math Game you simply do basic arithmetic (addition, subtraction and multiplication) while in the English Game you must unjumble the words presented above.
